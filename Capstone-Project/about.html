About Me

My name is Caitlyn Dickman, and I am a senior at Craig High School. I am a member of National Art Honors Society, Chamber Choir, and social media manager for Tri-Music Honors Society. As well as that I am president of Choir Leadership and have been in Acapella Choir for three years and in the choir department my entire high school career. I am a percussionist for Wind Ensemble, and have taken part in community and high school theatre, as well as Spotlighters Show Choir.

Career

My career goals are to possibly run a theatre or a business, and teach on the side. I have been accepted into Carthage's music department for musical theatre, and still have to audition at Whitewater (as those are my top two choices as of right now).
